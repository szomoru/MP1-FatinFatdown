What is Ketogenic Diet?

The keto diet is a very low-carb, moderate protein, high-fat diet. While you eat few gramms of carbohydrates on a keto diet, you maintain a moderate protein intake and an increased healthy fat consumption. The reduction of the carbs puts your body in a metabolic state that is called ketosis. During this state your body using your fat as energy source.
